# AirGuard
AirGuard is a mobile application designed to monitor and provide real-time data on air quality in major cities and industrial areas in Russia
The AirGuard project will initially be launched in major Russian cities and industrial hubs where air pollution is a significant concern. These locations include:

Moscow: As the capital and largest city, Moscow faces high levels of vehicular and industrial pollution.

Saint Petersburg: Another major urban center with increasing pollution due to traffic and industry.

Norilsk: Known for its high levels of industrial pollution, particularly from mining and metallurgy.

Yekaterinburg: An industrial city in the Ural region that suffers from air quality issues.


The scope of the project will focus on urban areas with significant pollution levels, expanding to other regions as the project grows. The aim is to cover both residential and industrial zones to protect citizens and raise awareness about air quality across Russia.
